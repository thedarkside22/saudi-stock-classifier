Tadawul Stock Direction Predictor

ML-based next-day direction prediction for Saudi-listed equities

Predicts the probability of a >1% price increase on the next trading day, based on features (RSI, ATR, Bollinger width, volatility, momentum) computed from recent OHLCV data. Random Forest classifier, ROC-AUC ~0.58 on held-out data. Not financial advice.
